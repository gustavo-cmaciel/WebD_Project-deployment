Program training for Beginners


Here's just a few reasons why you should incorporate strength training for beginners

1 You burn more calories. Lifting weights, whether they're barbells or dumbbells, boosts your metabolism, which in turn burns calories, which of course means fat loss. Of course, make sure you're properly fueling your body with protein so that your work building muscle doesn't go to waste! Gym workouts and nutrition go hand in hand.
2 It protects bone health and muscle mass. As we age, we lose muscle mass and bone density, so strength training can help you combat the effects of aging to make your bones, joints, and muscles healthier and stronger.
3 It can help improve day-to-day activities. Not only does strength training make you stronger, it also can help improve your balance, coordination, posture, and even flexibility, making daily activities easier and bettering your quality of life.
4 Doubles as a pick-me-up. Skip the afternoon cup of coffee and lift some weights instead! Research shows that strength training can elevate and increase the amount of endorphins, which can boost your energy and mood. And on the flipside, it can support better sleep at night, too!
