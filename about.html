About Us

Since 2001

It all started in 2001, when three friends and young entrepreneurs had an innovative idea, to make people healthier, in the face of the numbers of sedentary lifestyles and worldwide obesity that only grow, they saw an opportunity for business, it was there that emerged the IGYm, which is not a simple gym but a complete solution for the customer, who can have access to exclusive workouts in the comfort of your home and also specialized support from a complete network of nutritionists. The business has gone through ups and downs since its inception, but with it has consolidated its effective model - unfortunately - in a delightful moment of confinement that we live in due to the 2019 pandemic, as people have been forced to change their routine.
